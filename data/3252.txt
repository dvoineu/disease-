Tick-borne Relapsing Fever

Fact sheet: Be aware of tick-borne relapsing fever (TBRF) pdf icon[PDF - 1
page]

Relapsing fever is bacterial infection that can cause recurring bouts of
fever, headache, muscle and joint aches, and nausea. There are three types of
relapsing fever:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * Tick-borne relapsing fever (TBRF)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * Louse-borne relapsing fever (LBRF)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * _Borrelia miyamotoi_ disease (sometimes called hard tick relapsing fever)

TBRF occurs in the western United States and is usually linked to sleeping in
rustic, rodent-infested cabins in the mountains. In Texas, TBRF is frequently
linked to cave exposures. LBRF is transmitted by the human body louse and
usually occurs in refugee settings in developing parts of the world.
_Borrelia miyamotoi_ disease occurs in the same places where Lyme disease is
found and is transmitted by the blacklegged tick.
