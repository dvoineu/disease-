### Vaccines Recommended for Travel and Some Specific Groups

BCG is a vaccine for TB. This vaccine is not widely used in the United States,
but it is often given to infants and small children in other countries where
TB is common. BCG vaccine does not always protect people from getting TB. If
you were vaccinated with BCG, you may have a positive reaction to a TB skin
test. This reaction may be due to the BCG vaccine itself or due to infection
with the TB bacteria. Your positive reaction is more likely to mean you have
