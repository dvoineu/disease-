# Directories

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

MedlinePlus provides links to directories to help you find libraries, health
professionals, services and facilities. NLM does not endorse or recommend the
organizations that produce these directories, nor the individuals or
organizations that are included in the directories.
