# Antibiotic / Antimicrobial Resistance

Minus

Related Pages

Antibiotic resistance is one of the biggest public health challenges of our
time. Each year in the U.S., at least 2.8 million people get an antibiotic-
resistant infection, and more than 35,000 people die. Fighting this threat is
a public health priority that requires a collaborative global approach across
sectors. CDC is working to combat this threat. Find out how you can help.
