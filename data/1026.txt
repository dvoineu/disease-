# STD Clinical Slides

Minus

Related Pages

The STD Clinical Slides depict symptoms of sexually transmitted diseases. The
first option under each disease below, "View or download in Microsoft
PowerPoint" is a ready to use PowerPoint 97 slide show with captions for each
photograph. The second option under each disease below "Download graphics
only", includes separate files for each photograph without captions. These
files could be used to create slide shows or documents.
