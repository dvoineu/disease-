# Waterborne Disease & Outbreak Surveillance

Minus

Related Pages

![3D bar graphs and pie
charts](/healthywater/surveillance/images/surveillance-banner2.jpg)

Surveillance, or tracking illness and injury, provides information that can
help prevent future disease and outbreaks.  Although most waterborne illnesses
are not part of recognized outbreaks, outbreak surveillance provides important
information on how germs, chemicals, or toxins spread, and which types of
water are linked to people getting sick. Waterborne disease and outbreak
investigations give us information that can be used to prevent contamination,
illness, and future disease outbreaks.
