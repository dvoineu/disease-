Walking and Walkable Communitiespdf icon [PDF-1.3MB]  
The Status Report summarizes the state of walking and walkability when the
Call to Action was released, as well as selected activities done since the
release to sustain the messages and promote the goals established by the U.S.
Surgeon General.

health, and will be used by the government to develop the second edition of
the Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans.
