# Stay informed with Emergency Partners Information Connection (EPIC)
Newsletters

Minus

Related Pages

Stay informed about ways to keep you and your loved ones safe when a public
health emergency happens. Sign up for EPIC newsletters and announcements.

Emergency Partners is now EPIC! Looking for the Emergency Partners newsletter?
Find the archive here.

![Epic Insider](/epic/images/designElement_epicInsider_sm.jpg)

EPIC Insider provides basic information about ways to stay safer and healthier
when facing disasters, disease outbreaks, and other health threats.

![Epic Exchange](/epic/images/designElement_epicExchange_sm.jpg)

EPIC Exchange provides information on learning opportunities, training
materials, and partner organization emergency response activities.

![Epic Alert](/epic/images/designElement_epicAlert_sm.jpg)

EPIC Alerts are notifications that bring you urgent information about how to
stay safer and healthier during a public health emergency.

![epic Extra](/epic/images/designElement_epicExtra_sm.jpg)

EPIC Extra provides timely news to help you stay informed about ongoing public
health emergencies.
