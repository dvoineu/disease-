## Summary

Having a long-term, or chronic, illness can disrupt your life in many ways.
You may often be tired and in pain. Your illness might affect your appearance
or your physical abilities and independence. You may not be able to work,
causing financial problems. For children, chronic illnesses can be
frightening, because they may not understand why this is happening to them.

These changes can cause stress, anxiety, and anger. If they do, it is
important to seek help. A trained counselor can help you develop strategies to
regain a feeling of control. Support groups might help, too. You will find
that you are not alone, and you may learn some new tips on how to cope.

You may be able to manage your illness better if learn more about it. It is
important to evaluate the information that you find, to make sure that it is
reliable. It is also important to find a health care provider that you can
trust.
