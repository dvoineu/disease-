Dengue

Traveling to the Caribbean for Spring Break? Dengue cases are on the rise.
Protect yourself.

Learn more

Dengue viruses are spread to people through the bite of an infected _Aedes_
species ( _Ae._ _aegypti_ or _Ae. albopictus_ ) mosquito. Dengue is common in
more than 100 countries around the world. Forty percent of the world's
population, about 3 billion people, live in areas with a risk of dengue.
Dengue is often a leading cause of illness in areas with risk.
