# Public Health Practice Stories from the Field

Minus

Related Pages

The following stories highlight successes, progress, and innovations of state,
tribal, and local health departments as well as national public health
organizations. Some stories feature the work of CDC-funded partners. The
stories describe--

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Specific steps taken
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Achievements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Lessons learned

The stories are grouped by topic. Click or tap the plus sign next to a topic
of interest to reveal links to relevant stories.

Public health professionals can read and share these stories and consider how
similar action could advance public health efforts in their jurisdiction.
