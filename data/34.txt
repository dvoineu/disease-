# Genital Herpes

Minus

Related Pages

Fact Sheet

![woman looking at tablet](/std/herpes/images/factsheet.jpg)

You can get genital herpes from an infected partner, even if your partner has
no herpes symptoms.

More

Statistics

![people and graph](/std/herpes/images/data.jpg)

Genital herpes is a common STD, and most people with genital herpes infection
do not know they have it.

More

Treatment

![woman and doctor](/std/herpes/images/treatment.jpg)

There is no cure for herpes, but medication is available to reduce symptoms
and make it less likely that you will spread herpes to a sex partner.
