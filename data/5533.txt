# Colorado Tick Fever (CTF)

Minus

Related Pages

![Hiking female - pacific
northwest](/coloradotickfever/images/gettyimages-971165460-170667a.jpg)

CTF is a rare viral disease spread by infected Rocky Mountain wood ticks in
the western United States and western Canada.
