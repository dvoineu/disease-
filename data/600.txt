## Summary

Every racial or ethnic group has specific health concerns. Differences in the
health of groups can result from:

    * Genetics
    * Environmental factors
    * Access to care
    * Cultural factors

On this page, you'll find links to health issues that affect Native Hawaiians
and Pacific Islanders.
