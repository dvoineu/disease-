Extreme Heat

![Construction worker wiping sweat in extreme
heat](/nceh/toolkits/images/extreme_heat_home.jpg)

The effects of rising temperatures and extreme heat result in numerous
illnesses and deaths each year.

Flooding

![flooding on road with road closed
sign](/nceh/toolkits/images/flooding_home.jpg)

Hurricanes

![Hurricanes](/nceh/toolkits/images/hurricane_home.jpg)

Radiation Emergencies

![people in hazmat suits standing behind a sign pointing to the
decontamination zone](/nceh/toolkits/images/radiation_home.jpg)

Learn how to protect yourself and your family in a radiation emergency.

Winter Weather

![winter weather snow](/nceh/toolkits/images/winterweather_home.jpg)

When winter temperatures drop significantly below normal, staying warm and
safe can become a challenge.
