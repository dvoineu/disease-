Data are for the U.S.

Morbidity

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Number of adults with diagnosed heart disease: 30.3 million
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Percent of adults with diagnosed heart disease: 12.1%

Source: Summary Health Statistics Tables for U.S. Adults: National Health
Interview Survey, 2018, Table A-1b, A-1c pdf icon[PDF - 137 KB]

Physician office visits

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Percent of office-based physician visits with coronary artery disease, ischemic heart disease, or history of myocardial infarction indicated on the medical record: 6.7%

Source: National Ambulatory Medical Care Survey: 2016 National Summary Tables,
table 18 pdf icon[PDF - 793 KB]

Emergency department visits

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Percent of visits to emergency departments with coronary artery disease, ischemic heart disease, or history of myocardial infarction indicated on the medical record: 6.1%

Source: National Hospital Ambulatory Medical Care Survey: 2017 Emergency
Department Summary Tables, table 13 pdf icon[PDF - 863 KB]

Source: Deaths: Final Data for 2017, tables 6, 7 pdf icon[PDF - 2 MB]
