# Learn More About Thalassemia

EspaÃ±ol (Spanish)

Minus

Related Pages

Thalassemia is an inherited (i.e., passed from parents to children through
genes) blood disorder caused when the body doesn't make enough of a protein
called hemoglobin, an important part of red blood cells. Thalassemia is a
treatable disorder that can be well-managed with blood transfusions and
chelation therapy. It is important for people with thalassemia to learn how to
stay healthy.
