**PFAS Exposure Assessment** pdf icon [PDF - 609 KB] - This fact sheet has
information about the PFAS exposure assessments that the Centers for Disease
Control and Prevention (CDC) and the Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease
Registry (ATSDR) will be conducting in communities near current or former
military bases and that are known to have had PFAS in their drinking water.

### Publications by Topic

book icon

Health Assessments and Consultations

Links to a range of ATSDR documents related to health assessments, health
consultations and exposure investigations conducted for hazardous waste sites.

hazard icon

Sites

Links to a range of ATSDR documents related to hazardous waste sites,
including those in the National Priorities List, also known as superfund
sites.

people icon

Exposure and Health Registries

Links to a range of ATSDR documents related to health registries including the
National ALS Registry and the Tremolite Asbestos Registry.

alert icon

Emergency Response

Links to a range of ATSDR documents related to emergency response including
resources for first responders.

people icon

Community Involvement

Links to a range of ATSDR document about community engagement and health
promotion, like the Principles of Community Engagement.

chart icon

Data Resources

Links to a range of ATSDR resources where toxicologist, health professionals,
community members, educators, and health care providers can find data.

hazard icon

Toxic Substances

Evaluations of hazardous substances and their potential health effects,
including ATSDR Toxicological Profiles and ToxFAQs™.

clothes icon

Education & Training

Links to online education and training for medical and public health
providers, health assessors, and other health and environmental professionals.

chat icon

Risk Communication

Links to risk communication resources and publications like ATSDR's "A Primer
on Health Risk Communication".

lab icon

Monitoring and Testing

Links to a range of ATSDR documents related to monitoring and testing
including hair testing and analysis.
