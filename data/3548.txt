These pages includes information about the estimated burden of influenza from
past seasons, including tables of the estimated influenza disease burden (and
95% credible interval [Cr I]) by age group. Information on how these estimates
are calculated can be found on CDC's web page How CDC Estimates the Burden of
Seasonal Influenza in the U.S.
