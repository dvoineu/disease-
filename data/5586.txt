# Fiscal Year 2019 Budget

Minus

Related Pages

CDC's National Center on Birth Defects and Developmental Disabilities (NCBDDD)
fiscal year (FY) 2019 appropriation includes **$155.56 million** for Child
Health and Development, Health and Development with Disabilities, and the
Public Health Approach to Blood Disorders.

Funds are currently supporting surveillance, research, and prevention
activities that are addressing issues with the greatest public health burden
and implementing strategies to improve health outcomes.

![Fiscal Year 2019 Budget Pie Chart, see details
below](/ncbddd/aboutus/budget/images/budget-pie-chart-2019.png)
