# Hemophilia

EspaÃ±ol (Spanish)

Related Pages

Hemophilia is usually an inherited bleeding disorder in which the blood does
not clot properly. People with hemophilia can live full lives and enjoy most
of the activities that other people do. If you have hemophilia, or know
someone who does, it's important to learn how to stay as healthy as possible.
