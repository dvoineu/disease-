## Summary

Vaginal problems are some of the most common reasons women go to the doctor.
They may have symptoms such as

    * Itching
    * Burning
    * Pain
    * Abnormal bleeding
    * Discharge

One common problem is vaginitis, an inflammation of the vagina. Other problems
that affect the vagina include sexually transmitted diseases, vaginal cancer,
and vulvar cancer. Treatment of vaginal problems depends on the cause.
