###  Social_round_instagram IG Overview

Instagram is an online mobile photo-sharing, video-sharing and social
networking service that enables users to take pictures and videos, apply
digital filters to them, and share them on a variety of social networking
services, such as Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr and Flickr.
