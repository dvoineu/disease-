CDC's Division of Cancer Prevention and Control (DCPC) pools the expertise of
medical officers, health economists, epidemiologists, behavioral scientists,
and other researchers to find ways to reduce the burden of cancer and
eliminate health disparities. Professional biographies of some DCPC scientists
are listed below.
