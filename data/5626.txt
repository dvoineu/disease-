### FASD Competency-Based Curriculum Development Guide for Medical and Allied
Health Education and Practice

![FASD Competency-Based Curriculum Development
Guide](/ncbddd/fasd/curriculum/Competency-Based-Curriculum-
Guide-2015-350px.jpg)external icon

##### Teaching Tools

_The FASD Competency-Based Curriculum Development Guide for Medical and Allied
Health Education and Practice, 2015_ is designed to improve prevention,
identification, and management of fetal alcohol spectrum disorders (FASDs) and
can be used to develop educational programs and materials in a range of
formats based on the needs of learners. This is a revised version of the guide
that was originally released in 2009. It is a product of the formerly funded
FASD Regional Training Centers and partners.

The guide can be viewed online or downloaded and saved as a PDF from the
following site:

http://www.mpfasdpic.org/causes/competency-based-curriculum-development-
guide/external icon

##### Curriculum Guide

The curriculum guide covers the following seven competencies for learning:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          1. Foundation
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          2. Alcohol Use Disorders
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          3. Alcohol Screening and Brief Intervention
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          4. Biological Effects of Alcohol on the Fetus
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          5. Screening, Diagnosis, and Assessment for Treatment Planning
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          6. Treatment Across the Life Span for Persons with Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          7. Ethical, Legal, and Policy Issues
