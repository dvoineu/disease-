# Contact Us

Minus

Related Pages

![Contact Us](/publichealthgateway/images/landingpage-banners/Banner-
ContactUs.jpg)

We support public health professionals in state, tribal, local, and
territorial health agencies. How can we help you?

Public Health Professionals

![Group of public health professionals](/publichealthgateway/images/public-
health-pro.jpg)

If you are a public health professional at a state, tribal, local, or
territorial health department

Please contact us directly by calling 1-404-698-9246

or

Email us at CSTLTSfeedback@cdc.gov

Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19)

![Coronavirus](/publichealthgateway/images/index/hero-images/coronavirus-
dark-1920x1080-medium_cropped.jpg)

For current information about coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19)

Please visit CDC's COVID-19 website or visit the Resources for State, Local,
Territorial, and Tribal Health Departments web page

or

Contact CDC-INFO

General Public

![Normal day crowd walking on busy
street](/publichealthgateway/images/ContactUs-generalpublic.jpg)

If you are a member of the general public

Please direct all inquiries to CDC-INFO at 1-800-CDC-INFO (1-800-232-4636)

or

Use this form

Public Health Associate Program

![phap_logo](/publichealthgateway/images/logos/phap_logo.jpg)

PHAP@cdc.gov or 1-404-498-0030
