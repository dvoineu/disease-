Other STDs

There are dozens of STDs. Some STDs, such as syphilis, gonorrhea, and
chlamydia, are spread mainly by sexual contact. Other diseases, including Zika
and Ebola, _can_ be spread sexually but are more often spread through ways
other than sex.
