# CDC Publications

Minus

Related Pages

![A stack of papers in folders.](/arthritis/images/publications/stack-of-
folders-and-papers-400.jpg)

A core function of CDC's arthritis program is to publish prevalence estimates
and provide information that characterizes the burden of arthritis in the
United States. This information is often published in the _Morbidity and
Mortality Weekly_ _Report_ ( _MMWR_ ) or peer-reviewed journals.

A variety of arthritis-related publications by CDC staff are available in this
section.
