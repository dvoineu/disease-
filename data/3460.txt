## Educational and Promotional Resources for Partners

The National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases (NCIRD)
encourages partners to use our educational resources and promotional materials
to help supplement vaccine conversations and outreach with parents, pregnant
women, and adults, as well as healthcare professionals.

Find resources for each group or topic, including toolkits, drop-in articles,
digital media tools, videos, PSAs, fact sheets, and social media messages.
