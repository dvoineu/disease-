Medscape Commentary Directory

This special series of commentariesexternal icon, interviews, and slideshows
is part of a collaboration between CDC and Medscape. This content is designed
to deliver CDC's authoritative guidance directly to Medscape's physicians,
nurses, pharmacists, and other healthcare professionals. Below are links to
commentaries (2014-present) about NCEZID infectious disease topics.
