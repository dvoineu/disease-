# Worker Health Study Summaries

Minus

Related Pages

### NIOSH research on long-term exposure

This page provides summaries of NIOSH worker health studies that have been
done over the years. These summaries, or worker notifications, were sent to
those workers who were included in each study.

If you have any questions about these study summaries, contact us by calling
800-CDC-INFO.

_NOTICE: Many of these are NIOSH Archive Documents, and may not represent
current NIOSH Policy._
