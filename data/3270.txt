Coronavirus Disease 2019

CDC is responding to the novel coronavirus outbreak.

![Person washing hands with soap and
water](/media/images/homepage/carousel/prevent-illness-900px.jpg)

Steps to Prevent Illness

The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to COVID-19.

![Woman with a fever checking her temperature with a
thermometer](/media/images/homepage/carousel/sick-900px.jpg)

What to Do if You are Sick

If you are sick with COVID-19 or suspect you are infected, take steps to help
prevent the disease from spreading.

![Neighborhood](/media/images/homepage/carousel/safe-communities-900px.jpg)

Keep Work, Schools, and Communities Safe

Protect yourself and your community from getting and spreading COVID-19.
