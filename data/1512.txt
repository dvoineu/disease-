# Learn about Specific Birth Defects

EspaÃ±ol (Spanish)

Minus

Related Pages

Major birth defects are structural changes in one or more parts of the body.
They are present at birth. They can have a serious, adverse effect on the
health, development, or functional ability of the baby.

Learn about diagnosing birth defects
