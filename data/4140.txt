# AsthmaStats

Minus

Related Pages

AsthmaStats provides national and state level estimates among children and
adults on asthma-related specific topics using data from National Center for
Health Statistics (NCHS) surveys, the Vital Statistics System, and the BRFSS
Asthma Call-back Survey (ACBS).

Asthma Attacks among People with Current Asthma, 2014-2017

 **Nearly half the people with current asthma had one or more asthma attacks
in the past 12 months**

In an asthma attack, the airways become inflamed, making breathing difficult.
On average, 50.2% of children and 44.7% of adults with current asthma had one
or more asthma attacks during 2014-2017\. The percentage of asthma attacks
varied by state. Among children, the median percentage was 51.0%, but it
ranged from 23.1% in Nebraska to 81.7% in Wyoming. Among adults, the median
percentage was 44.8%, but it ranged from 32.7% in North Dakota to 66.6% in
Alaska.
