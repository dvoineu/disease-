# Grants/Funding

Minus

Related Pages

Funding opportunities are provided for the overall promotion of global health
and disease prevention.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Office of Financial Resources  
Discover the amount the CDC awards in grant and contract actions, including
simplified acquisitions. Also provided is information on grants and business
opportunities with the CDC.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * CDC Budget  
Learn about the CDC budget which consists of the Working Capital Fund (WCF)
and the Prevention and Public Health Fund. See also congressional
justifications.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * NIH Grants and Funding OpportunitiesExternal  
(National Institutes of Health)  
Find out about the many grants and funding opportunities available from the
National Institutes of Health (NIH).

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Use of Vaccine Purchased with 317 Funds  
Answers to questions about this Act that authorizes the federal purchase of
vaccines to vaccinate children, adolescents, and adults to mostly meet the
needs of underinsured children not eligible for VFC, and uninsured adults.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Biden Announces Recovery Act Funds to Help Care for Children, Prevent Disease: Press ReleaseExternal  
The Obama Administration announced that $300 million from the American
Recovery and Reinvestment Act (ARRA) would be used to ensure more underserved
Americans receive the vaccines they need. The majority of these new resources
will be used to purchase vaccines, which will be distributed through the
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention's Section 317 immunization program
to all 50 states, several large cities, and U.S. territories.
