### **Surveillance Summaries**

* * *

March 27, 2020  
Prevalence of Autism Spectrum Disorder Among Children Aged 8 Years -- Autism
and Developmental Disabilities Monitoring Network, 11 Sites, United States,
2016  
Report findings indicate that there continue to be many children living with
autism who need services and support, now and as they grow into adolescence
and adulthood. Screening and awareness continue to identify children who will
benefit from services.
