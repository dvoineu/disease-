**Due to the high demand we anticipate for this COCA Call, we encourage
participants to consider viewing on Facebook. To see the call live, please
click onCOCA's Facebook Page.**

#### **Overview**

During this COCA call, presenters will focus on current information about
people who are higher risk for COVID-19 complications because of their age or
underlying medical conditions, particularly those that are not well-
controlled.
