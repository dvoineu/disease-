Minus

## Welcome to Atlanta

Atlanta is an exhilarating city that offers residents and visitors world-class
options in dining, accommodations, and entertainment. Although a metropolis,
the city boasts of diverse neighborhoods, great school systems and, of course,
southern hospitality. Find out more about Living in Atlantaexternal icon

## Relocation Information

Deciding to move for a career opportunity is a decision that affects the
entire family. Climate, commute time and access to amenities and services, are
some factors to consider before accepting an offer. To make the transition
easier, we have compiled a list of websites that provide comprehensive
relocation information for each duty station.

If you are assigned to another field location, be sure to check your Center,
Institute or Office's (CIO) Web site on CDC.gov to acquaint yourself with the
resources provided, such as office locations and contact information. A
general Web search will provide a variety of relocation information for each
site.



**International Locations**

If you are assigned to a position outside of the Continental United States, it
is important you visit the U.S. State Department Consular Information
Programexternal icon  page to view conditions abroad that may affect your
safety and security. For information about travel to and living in your host
nation, visit Government Visa Travelexternal icon, Traveler's Health, and the
CIA's World Factbookexternal icon.
