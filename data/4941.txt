coordinates funding opportunities to help strengthen public health systems and
services.

Learn More

PHHS Block Grant

The Preventive Health and Health Services (PHHS) Block Grant provides federal
funding for 61 recipients: all 50 states, the District of Columbia, 2 American
Indian tribes, 5 US territories, and 3 freely associated states.

Visit Site
