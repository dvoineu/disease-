# Petrositis

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

Petrositis most often refers to infection of the temporal bone, the bone in
the skull that surrounds the ear. This infection goes deep to the inner ear.
It is sometimes associated with otitis media (a middle ear infection).
