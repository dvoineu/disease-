Statistics

![Photo of a person looking at statistics about the United States on a digital
tablet](/cancer/prostate/images/usmap-tablet-300x200.jpg)

Uterine cancer is the most common gynecologic cancer in the United States. The
Data Visualizations Tool provides detailed statistics.

_Inside Knowledge_

![Photo of a woman](/cancer/gynecologic/images/ik-300x200.jpg)

CDC's _Inside Knowledge_ campaign raises awareness of the five main
gynecologic cancers: cervical, ovarian, uterine, vaginal, and vulvar.
