# NIOSH Science Awards

Minus

Related Pages

![awards 2018 booklet](/niosh/awards/images/awards2018.jpg)

The NIOSH Science Awards are presented annually to recognize significant
accomplishments in research, partnership, research translation, career
achievements, and service.

Information about the 2019 Science Award nominees and winners can be found in
