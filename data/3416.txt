# Welcome to the CDC Library

Minus

Related Pages

The mission of the Stephen B. Thacker CDC Library is to provide the scientific
foundation for CDC's mission by offering specialized library services
supporting research, information exchange and learning. Through the
headquarters library in Atlanta and its four branches, CDC Library serves CDC
employees across the nation as well as those located internationally.
