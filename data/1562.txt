### Preventing Birth Defects

![Folic Acid materials collage](/ncbddd/birthdefects/documents/Folic-Acid-
materials.jpg)

Folic Acid  
Folic acid can help prevent major birth defects of the baby's brain and spine.
CDC has materials for women, healthcare providers, and others on the benefits
of folic acid.

* * *

![Alcohol and Pregnancy Fact Sheet](/ncbddd/birthdefects/documents/alcohol-
pregnancy-factsheet.jpg)

Alcohol Use in Pregnancy  
Drinking alcohol during pregnancy can cause miscarriage, stillbirth, and a
range of lifelong disorders, known as fetal alcohol spectrum disorders
(FASDs). CDC has materials for women, healthcare providers, and others on
alcohol use in pregnancy and FASDs.
