To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

The U.S. National Library of Medicine (NLM) created these animated videos to
explain topics in health and medicine, and to answer frequently asked
questions about diseases, health conditions, and wellness issues. They feature
research from the National Institutes of Health (NIH), presented in language
you can understand. Each video page includes links to MedlinePlus health topic
pages, where you can find more information about the subject, including
symptoms, causes, treatment, and prevention.
