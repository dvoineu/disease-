# Health Check Tools

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

Assess your health with interactive tools such as calculators, quizzes, and
questionnaires. You can check your risk for heart disease, calculate your
ideal weight and body mass, find out how many calories your favorite exercise
burns, and more.
