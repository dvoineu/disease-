# Definitions of Health Terms

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

Staying healthy includes exercising, eating right, and learning how to take
care of your overall well-being. To help you, MedlinePlus offers these easy-
to-read definitions of common health words and phrases. These will also be
helpful when you are talking with your health care provider.
