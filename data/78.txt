### Report to Congress on The Management of TBI in Children

CDC defines a traumatic brain injury (TBI) as a disruption in the normal
function of the brain that can be caused by a bump, blow, or jolt to the head,
or penetrating head injury. Everyone is at risk for a TBI, especially children
and older adults.
