### Lupus awareness

Lupus is a disease that can affect people of all ages, races, and ethnicities.
The signs and symptoms vary. Learn more.

![Five young people](/lupus/images/09202018_1185x350.jpg)

### What is Lupus?

Lupus occurs when an unknown trigger causes a person's own immune system to
attack their tissues, damaging the tissues and producing widespread
inflammation. Learn More.
