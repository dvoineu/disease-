# _Chlamydia pneumoniae_ Infection

Minus

Related Pages

![woman sneezing](/pneumonia/atypical/cpneumoniae/images/woman-sneezing.jpg)

_Chlamydia pneumoniae_ is a type of bacteria that can cause respiratory tract
infections, such as pneumonia. _C. pneumoniae_ is one cause of community-
acquired pneumonia or lung infections developed outside of a hospital.
However, not everyone exposed to _C. pneumoniae_ will develop pneumonia.
