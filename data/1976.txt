## Summary

The vulva is the external part of a woman's genitals. Some problems you can
have with the vulvar area include

    * Vaginitis or vulvovaginitis, swelling or infection of the vulva and vagina
    * Skin problems due to allergy 
    * Vulvar cancer
    * Vulvodynia, or vulvar pain

Symptoms may include redness, itching, pain, or cracks in the skin. Treatment
depends on the cause.
