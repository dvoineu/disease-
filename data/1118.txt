### 2017 High School YRBS Slides

The 2017 Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System (YRBSS) results are
summarized in a PowerPoint® presentation that presents national prevalence
estimates by sex, grade, and race/ethnicity, national trends, state and local
medians and ranges, and state maps.
