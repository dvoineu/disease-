# MedlinePlus Connect: Email List

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

MedlinePlus Connect is a free service of the National Library of Medicine
(NLM), National Institutes of Health (NIH), and the Department of Health and
Human Services (HHS). This service allows health organizations or health IT
providers to link patient portals or electronic health record (EHR) systems to
MedlinePlus, an authoritative up-to-date health information resource for
patients, families, and health care providers.

Join the MedlinePlus Connect discussion list to keep up with developments and
exchange ideas with your colleagues. This email list will be useful for health
IT developers and other interested users.

To subscribe to the list, send an email to LISTSERV@LIST.NIH.GOV with the
following text in the message body: Subscribe MEDLINEPLUSCONNECT Your First
and Last Name

For example: Subscribe MEDLINEPLUSCONNECT Jane Smith

Please tell us if you implement MedlinePlus Connect by contacting us or
posting to the email discussion list.
