# Medical Tests

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

Learn about medical tests, including what the tests are used for, why a doctor
may order a test, how a test will feel, and what the results may mean.Â

Medical tests can help detect a condition, determine a diagnosis, plan
treatment, check to see if treatment is working, or monitor the condition over
time **.** A doctor may order these tests as part of a routine checkup, to
check for certain diseases and disorders, or to monitor your health.
