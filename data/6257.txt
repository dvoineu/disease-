# US Outbreaks of Zoonotic Diseases Spread between Animals & People

Minus

Related Pages

Below is a selected list from CDC of outbreaks of human infections linked to
contact with animals and animal products in the United States. This list is
not comprehensive, and outbreaks may have occurred that are not included here.
