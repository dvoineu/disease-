# National Disease Intervention Specialists (DIS) Recognition Day

Minus

Related Pages

October 4, 2019

DIS remain the backbone of public health in the United States. They wear many
hats and take on many roles to protect people across the nation. Whether
tackling STD prevention, tuberculosis outbreak response, HIV exposure
notification, or emergency response - DIS bring a special set of skills and
level of dedication matched by few in the field. We are honored for the
opportunities to work alongside them and to call them our colleagues. Today,
we honor you, DIS, and all that you do in the name of public health.
