## Summary

We all want high-quality health care, but it's hard to know how to choose.
There are many things to consider, including

    * What your insurance covers
    * Whether a health care provider or service is accredited
    * The location of a service
    * Hours that the service is available
    * Whether you like a health care provider's personality 

On this page you'll find information to help you choose a health care provider
or service.
