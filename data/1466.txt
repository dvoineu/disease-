What is Legionnaires' disease?

Legionnaires' (LEE-juh-nares) disease is a serious type of pneumonia (lung
infection) caused by _Legionella_ (LEE-juh-nell-a) bacteria. People can get
sick when they breathe in mist or accidently swallow water into the lungs
containing _Legionella_.
