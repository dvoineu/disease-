## TB Highlight of the Month

This month's highlight features the **_Screening for Latent TB Infection
(LTBI) video,_ **from the Global Tuberculosis Institute at Rutgers, The State
University of New Jersey. The video provides information on the basics of TB
infection, targeted testing, recommendations for screening and testing high-
risk groups, and patient education messages. The video webpage contains
additional resources for primary care providers and patients.
