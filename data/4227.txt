### White House Reports

The White House ordered federal-level reports to analyze the oversight of
laboratory work involving select agents and toxins and identify ways to
strengthen policies and practices both at a national level and as a part of
the FSAP.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Report of the Federal Experts Security Advisory Panelpdf iconexternal icon, or FESAP.  
A report and recommendations developed by a federal advisory panel.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          * Fast Track Action Committee Report: Recommendations on the Select Agent Regulationspdf iconexternal icon, or FTAC-SAR.  
A report and recommendations informed by external stakeholder input.
