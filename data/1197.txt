6 Steps to Prevent COVID-19

6 Steps to Prevent COVID-19

Cancer patients treated with chemotherapy are more likely to get an infection.
That's because chemotherapy can weaken the immune system. Take steps to lower
your risk of getting sick with COVID-19. This video shows some things you
should do.
