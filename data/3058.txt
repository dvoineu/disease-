Emergency Preparedness for Older Adults and Caregivers

This portal provides links to information, tools, and resources to assist in
multi-sector planning for older adults in all-hazard emergencies.

**CDC Features:** Follow these easy steps to make sure you're protected

Identifying Vulnerable Older Adults

![Identifying Vulnerable Older Adults
cover](/aging/emergency/images/cover.jpg)

**_Identifying Vulnerable Older Adults and Legal Options for Increasing Their
Protection During All-Hazards Emergencies: A Cross-Sector Guide for States and
Communities_** covers topic areas such as developing plans, partnering and
collaboration, using data for action, building registries, using law-based
solutions, sheltering, and caregiver preparedness.
