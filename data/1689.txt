Learn what you can do to manage your epilepsy or help a loved one manage their
epilepsy.

Managing Epilepsy Well Checklist

Practice these self-management strategies to better control your seizures and
your health.

Parent Toolkit

A support program for parents of teens who have epilepsy.

Find Support

Free resources to help you or someone you love manage living with epilepsy.

Clinical Trials

Learn more about clinical trials and find clinical trials that focus on
epilepsy.

Help Someone Else Manage Seizures

Successful self-management requires an active partnership between a person
with epilepsy, the person's health care provider, and family and friends.

Epilepsy Self-Management Research

The Managing Epilepsy Well (MEW) Network is a group of academic Prevention
Research Centers that conduct studies related to epilepsy self-management.

Read about MEW Network projects and how they are improving health and quality
of life through their research, programs, and tools.
