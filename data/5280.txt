This page provides information on selected public health emergency topics of
recent public interest. For a list of the latest updates posted on this web
site, please see the What's New page.

![XDR Tuberculosis](/recentincidents/images/recent_xdrtb.jpg)

MDR Tuberculosis (December 2007)  
CDC is currently working on an investigation involving an international
traveler to the U.S. recently diagnosed with multidrug-resistant tuberculosis
(MDR TB).

![Photo of ground after an
earthquake.](/recentincidents/images/recent_earthquake.jpg)

Earthquake (October 31, 2007)  
A magnitude-5.6 tremor, centered about 9 miles northeast of San Jose, hit the
San Francisco Bay area. CDC reminds you that by planning and practicing what
to do if an earthquake strikes, you and your family can learn to react
correctly and automatically when the shaking begins.

![Photo of firefighter at
wildfire.](/recentincidents/images/recent_wildfires.jpg)

Wildfires (October 2007)  
Learn how to stay safe when wildfires are a threat.

![Salmonella](/recentincidents/images/recent_salmonella_4512eyeminus.jpg)

Salmonella (October 2007)  
CDC is collaborating with public health officials in multiple states across
the United States and with the U.S. Department of Agriculture Food Safety and
Inspection Service to investigate an ongoing multi-state outbreak of
Salmonella infections in humans.

![E. coli](/recentincidents/images/recent_ecoli_O157H7.jpg)

_E. coli_ (September-October 2007)  
Several state health departments, CDC, and the USDA are investigating a
multistate outbreak of _E. coli_ O157:H7 infections. On September 29, USDA
issued a notice to recall of 21.7 millions pounds of frozen ground beef
patties. Learn more about what CDC is doing about this recent outbreak.

![Salmonella](/recentincidents/images/recent_salmonella_drypetfood.jpg)

Salmonella (August-September 2007)  
Learn more about what CDC is doing to investigate the recent outbreak of
_Salmonella_ serotype Schwarzengrund associated with dry pet food.

![Anthrax](/recentincidents/images/recent_anthrax_skins.jpg)

Anthrax (September 2007)  
Recent anthrax cases linked to exposure to animal skins have spurred interest
in how you can be exposed to anthrax.

![Satellite photo of
hurricane.](/recentincidents/images/recent_hurricane1.jpg)

Tropical Storms Dean and Erin  
Learn how to keep you and your family safe during a tropical storm or
hurricane.

![Asbestos](/recentincidents/images/recent_do_not_cross.jpg)

Bridge Collapse  
Find resources related to coping with and treating injuries related to the
Minneapolis bridge collapse.

![Asbestos](/recentincidents/images/recent_asbestos01.jpg)

Asbestos  
Asbestos found in the debris from a recent steam pipe explosion in midtown
Manhattan has raised concern about the danger of exposure to asbestos. Learn
more about the health effects of asbestos exposure.

![Botulism](/recentincidents/images/recent_botulism02.jpg)

Botulism  
Learn more about botulism associated with canned chili sauce (July 2007)

![Blast Injuries](/recentincidents/images/recent_blastinjuries.jpg)

Blast Injuries  
Recent events in London have renewed interest in the sort of injuries that
result from car bombs. Learn more about blast injuries.

![Salmonella](/recentincidents/images/recent_salmonella_062807.jpg)

Salmonella Wandsworth Outbreak (June-July 2007)  
Learn more about the recent outbreak of a rare type of salmonella associated
with a popular vegetarian snack food.

![ecoli](/recentincidents/images/recent_beef_recall.jpg)

Information related to April-June 2007 ground beef recallsexternal icon  
Find out which products have been recalled and what you should do.

![XDR Tuberculosis](/recentincidents/images/recent_xdrtb.jpg)

XDR Tuberculosis (May 2007)  
Recent events involving a U.S. air traveler have sparked interest in this rare
type of TB that is resistant to almost all drugs. Learn more about this
disease.

![Photo of firefighter at
wildfire.](/recentincidents/images/recent_wildfires.jpg)

Wildfires (March 2007)  
Learn how to stay safe when wildfires are a threat.

![Thallium](/recentincidents/images/recent_chemical.jpg)

Thallium (March 2007)  
Recent events involving American tourists in Russia have focused attention on
Thallium. Learn how to recognize symptoms of toxic metal poisoning.

![Acanthamoeba Infection](/recentincidents/images/recent_acanthamoeba.jpg)

Acanthamoeba Infection  
Learn more about the serious eye infections associated with soft contact lens
solution.

![Salmonella](/recentincidents/images/recent_salmonella.jpg)

_Salmonella_ Outbreak (Aug 2006-present)  
Learn more about the current outbreak associated with peanut butter.

Page last reviewed: September 29, 2011
