# Join a developer community with CDC Open Technology to explore open source
data, code and APIs

![hero api image](/assets/img/img-hero-intro.svg)

## Open Technology

As the nation's health protection agency, CDC saves lives and protects people
from health threats. We also strive to be open with our public health data
sets, APIs and code repositories. By sharing CDC resources, we hope to foster
innovation and increase the health security of our nation.
