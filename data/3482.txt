National Infant Immunization Week (NIIW)

![National Infant Immunization Week, Power to
Protect](/vaccines/events/niiw/promotional/print-materials/images/logos-
icons/jpg/niiw-logo-color-english-thmb.jpg)

Annual observance to promote the benefits of immunizations and to focus on the
importance of immunizing infants against vaccine-preventable diseases by age
two.

National Immunization Awareness Month (NIAM)

![National Immunization Awareness Month
#ivax2protect](/vaccines/events/images/promo-niam-logo_sample-sm.png)

While CDC does not sponsor this month, CDC does support and encourage the
efforts of state and local health departments and other immunization partners
to celebrate NIAM and encourage all people to protect their health by being
immunized against infectious diseases.

More

National Influenza Vaccination Week (NIVW)

![It's National Influenza Vaccination Week \(NIVW\) CDC
#fightflu](/vaccines/events/images/nivw-logo.png)

This event highlights the importance of continuing influenza (flu) vaccination
through the winter months and beyond.

National Immunization Conference (NIC)

alert iconAs COVID-19 response activities intensify nationwide, we are
**canceling the National Immunization Conference (NIC)** , scheduled for May
19-21, 2020 in Atlanta, GA.  While we regret any inconvenience this may cause,
we recognize the current challenges facing the public health community in
multiple jurisdictions and the need to protect the health of meeting
participants.
