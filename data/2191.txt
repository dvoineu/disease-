# Information for Trainers and Librarians

To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.

The goal of MedlinePlus is to present high-quality, relevant health and
wellness information that is trusted, easy to understand, and free of
advertising, in both English and Spanish.

We appreciate your efforts in teaching people how to use MedlinePlus. Here are
some training resources which might help you with your classes and outreach
activities.
