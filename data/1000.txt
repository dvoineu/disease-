# Practical Use of Program Evaluation among Sexually Transmitted Disease (STD)
Programs

Minus

Related Pages

This manual provides step-by-step guidance on how to design and implement a
program evaluation. Its goal is to build the evaluation capacity of STD
programs so that they can internally monitor their program activities,
understand what is working or not working, and improve their efforts.

**To print in brochure layout** , set printer to duplex (Print on Both Sides)
and landscape orientation. Fold brochure in half and staple in center fold.
