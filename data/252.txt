## Summary

Health care workers are exposed to many job hazards. These can include

  * Infections
  * Needle injuries
  * Back injuries
  * Allergy-causing substances
  * Violence
  * Stress

Follow good job safety and injury prevention practices. They can reduce your
risk of health problems. Use protective equipment, follow infection control
guidelines, learn the right way to lift heavy objects, and find ways to manage
stress.

National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health
