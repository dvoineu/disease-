Basic Information

![Photo of a man and a woman](/cancer/flu/images/couple-394.jpg)

Cancer patients and survivors have a higher risk of complications from the
flu. We answer important questions.

Families and Caregivers

![A nurse gives a flu shot to a teen girl while her sister, mother, and
grandmother watch](/cancer/flu/images/phil9410-394.jpg)

It's important for cancer patients' caregivers and family members to take
steps to prevent flu from spreading.

Flu Treatment

![A woman picks up her prescription at a
pharmacy](/cancer/flu/images/prescription-394.jpg)

Antiviral drugs can make your illness milder, shorten the time you are sick,
and prevent serious flu complications.

Prevent Flu!

Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) 124K subscribers Prevent Flu!
Get a Flu Vaccine and Take Preventive Actions
