About NCHHSTP

The National Center's vision is a future free of HIV/AIDS, viral hepatitis,
STDs, and TB. NCHHSTP is responsible for:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Public health surveillance
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Prevention research
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Programs to prevent and control HIV/AIDS, other STDs, viral hepatitis, TB
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  * Promoting school-based health and disease prevention among youth
