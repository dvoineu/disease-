Address environmental causes of foodborne illnesses with our free food safety
resources below.

Tools and Guidance

National Environmental Assessment Reporting System (NEARS)

![Image of the cover of the NEARS manual](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/nears-
cover-short.jpg)

Is your jurisdiction registered? Capture environmental assessment data from
foodborne illness outbreaks to help prevent outbreaks associated with
restaurants and other food venues.

Infographics

![Graphic image of a chef.](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/chef-half.png)

**Infographic on Cooking Chicken Liver** - Learn the importance of cooking
chicken liver to 165°F.

**Infographic on Contributing Factors** - Learn about contributing factors to
outbreaks and how you can identify them during outbreak investigations.

**Infographic on Kitchen Manager Certification** - Learn more about restaurant
food safety and the importance of kitchen manager certification.

**Infographic on Preventing Foodborne Illness** - Learn how we help prevent
foodborne illness outbreaks by understanding the environmental factors
contributing to them.

Findings & Recommendations in Plain Language

![Image of the cover of the NEARS
manual](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/more.jpg)

Peruse our research findings and recommendations on restaurant food safety
topics such as sick food workers, hand hygiene, and kitchen manager
certification.

Tools from Funded Health Department Partners

![Photo of raw chicken being cut properly.](/nceh/ehs/ehsnet/images/raw-
chicken-prep-small.png)

Find tools from funded health department partners working to reduce foodborne
illness and outbreaks in their communities; for example, multilingual training
materials for restaurants on how to prevent campylobacter.

Training

Environmental Assessment Training Series (EATS)

![EATS logo is a circle with EATS in the middle and 3
stars](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/eats-140.png)

Practice skills in an interactive virtual environment and learn to conduct
environmental assessments as part of outbreak investigations.

Environmental Health Training in Emergency Response (EHTER)

![EHTER](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/EHTER.jpg)

Improve your knowledge, skills, and resources to address the environmental
health impacts of emergencies and disasters on food safety and other topics.

Environmental Public Health Online Courses (EPHOC)

![EPHOC instructor screen](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/EPHOC.jpg)

Access comprehensive workforce development resources for environmental public
health practitioners with this free package of 15 e-learning courses on food
protection and other topics.

Research

Can Restaurant Managers Talk with Sick Workers? 3 Things Restaurant Managers
Need To Know

![Two food workers talking to each other.](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/talk-w-
sick-workers.jpg)

Managers need to know if their workers are sick so they can decide if they
should handle food. Do the Food Code, HIPAA, and ADA address this topic?

Environmental Health Specialists Network (EHS-Net)

![Photo of tomatoes in plastic
bins.](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/tomatoes.jpg)

More than half of foodborne illness outbreaks are associated with restaurants.
Learn about our research program to identify environmental causes of outbreaks
in the restaurant setting.

Foods Linked to Foodborne Illness Outbreaks

![Image of a thermometer and a piece of raw
chicken.](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/therm-chicken.jpg)

Access studies on how restaurants handle specific foods (e.g., beef, poultry,
eggs, tomatoes, leafy greens) associated with outbreaks.

Practices Linked to Foodborne Illness Outbreaks

![Image of a chef speaking with a kitchen
manager.](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/chef_w_mgr.jpg)

Explore studies on restaurant food safety practices (food cooling, food
preparation, handwashing, sick workers, kitchen manager certification, and
others).

Other Food Safety Studies

![2 couples dining in a restaurant](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/couples-
dining.jpg)

Review studies on outbreak characteristics and other topics.

Investigations

Environmental Investigation of a Multistate _E. coli_ Outbreak on Spinach

![photo of a bunch of spinach](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/spinach-bunch.jpg)

Read about our watershed-scale investigation of the outbreak, including the
potential effects of irrigation water quality.

Food Safety Publications

![Image of a shelf of publications](/nceh/ehs/activities/images/pubs-
shelf.jpg)

Explore our food safety publications.
