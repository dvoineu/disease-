# Gonorrhea

Minus

Related Pages

![Anyone who is sexually active can get
gonorrhea.](/std/gonorrhea/images/gono-4.jpg)

Anyone who is sexually active can get gonorrhea.

Fact Sheet

![hands and tablet computer](/std/gonorrhea/images/factsheet.jpg)

Sexually active women younger than 25 years or women with new or multiple sex
partners, or a sex partner who has an STD should be tested every year.

More

Antibiotic Resistance

![gonorrhea bacterium](/std/gonorrhea/images/arg.jpg)

Gonorrhea has progressively developed resistance to the antibiotic drugs
prescribed for treatment.

More

Treatment

![man and woman talking to a doctor](/std/gonorrhea/images/treatment.jpg)

Gonorrhea can be cured with the right medication. Untreated gonorrhea can
cause serious health problems in both women and men.

More
