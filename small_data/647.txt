# Frequently Asked Questions

Minus

Related Pages

**How can I subscribe to a CDC Newsletter?**  
If you are interested in receiving information from CDC, please subscribe and
select from our list of newsletters.  Your email address will only be used to
deliver the requested information and to give you access to your user profile.

**How can I manage my newsletter subscriptions?**  
You can access Manage Your Subscriptions to add or remove newsletter
subscription topics.

**I am not seeing emails from CDC.  
**To ensure delivery of email updates to your inbox, please add
**emailupdates@cdc.gov**  to your email address book, adjust your spam
settings, or follow the instructions from your email provider on how to
prevent emails from being marked as "Spam" or "Junk Mail."

**I want to unsubscribe from all topics.   How can I do that?**

You can access Manage Your Subscriptions to remove all your newsletter
subscription topics.

**Help with CDC 's Email Service  
**If you need assistance with CDC's email service, please contact
subscriptions@cdc.gov or call CDC at 1-800-232-4636.
