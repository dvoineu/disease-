### CHOICES Intervention

![CHOICES Curriculum CD Package](/ncbddd/fasd/images/choices-curruculum-cd-
package-135.jpg)

**CHOICES Curriculum ―CD Package**  
CHOICES: A Program for Women About Choosing Healthy Behaviors is an
intervention for nonpregnant women of childbearing age and is designed to
prevent alcohol-exposed pregnancies by addressing risky drinking and
ineffective or no use of contraception.

The CHOICES Curriculum includes CD-ROMs for each of the following: Facilitator
guide, counselor manual, client workbook, and a training DVD with videos
demonstrating components of the intervention.

These materials are designed for use by professionals trained in behavioral
health counseling who will be conducting the CHOICES program. Please preview
the materials to see if they are appropriate for your purposes before
ordering.  
Facilitator Guide pdf icon[8 MB, 156 Pages, Print Only]  
Counselor Manual pdf icon[2 MB, 72 Pages, Print Only]  
Client Workbook pdf icon[1 MB, 54 Pages, Print Only]

![ CHOICES Intervention Fact
Sheet](/ncbddd/fasd/images/CHOICES_OnePager_thumb-135px.jpg)

CHOICES Intervention Fact Sheet pdf icon[229 KB, 2 Pages, 508]  
This double-sided sheet describes the risks of alcohol-exposed pregnancies,
how these risks can be reduced through the CHOICES intervention, and how the
intervention research has been translated into and improves public health
practice. It also provides responses to frequently asked questions, lists
citations for related publications, and tells readers where to go for more
information.

### Screening & Brief Intervention

![Screening & Brief
Intervention](/ncbddd/fasd/images/screeningbriefingintervention135.jpg)

Planning and Implementing Screening and Brief Intervention for Risky Alcohol
Use: A Step-by-Step Guide for Primary Care Practices pdf icon[2 MB, 52 Pages,
Print Only]  
This guide provides a process and resources to help staff in any primary care
practice to plan and implement alcohol screening and brief intervention (SBI)
to reduce alcohol use, an evidence-based approach recommended for all adults,
including pregnant women. The guide also presents information on risky alcohol
use and how it can be addressed through alcohol SBI.

![Planning and Implementing Screening and Brief Intervention for Risky Alcohol
Use: A Step-by-Step Guide for Tribal Communities](/ncbddd/fasd/images/Step-by-
Step-Guide-Tribal-Communities-thumb-135px.jpg)

Planning and Implementing Screening and Brief Intervention for Risky Alcohol
Use: A Step-by-Step Guide for Tribal Communities pdf icon[2 MB, 62 Pages,
Print Only]

This guide was adapted from _Planning and Implementing Screening and Brief
Intervention for Risky Alcohol Use: A Step-by-Step Guide for Primary Care
Practices_ to improve cultural relevance for American Indian/Alaska Native
communities.

### Curriculum Development

![FASD Competency-Based Curriculum Development Guide for Medical and Allied
Health Education and Practice](/ncbddd/fasd/images/FASD-Competency-Based-
Curriculum-135.jpg)

FASD Competency-Based Curriculum Development Guide for Medical and Allied
Health Education and Practiceexternal icon  
This curriculum development guide is designed to improve prevention,
identification, and management of FASDs and can be used to develop educational
programs and materials in a range of formats based on the needs of learners.
Based on seven core competencies, it is intended for use with medical and
allied health students and practitioners.

### Guidelines & Recommendations

![FAS Guidelines](/ncbddd/fasd/images/FAS-Guidelines-135.gif)

FAS Guidelines for Referral and Diagnosis pdf icon[510 KB, 62 Pages, Print
Only]  
These guidelines are intended to assist physicians and allied health
professionals in the timely identification, referral, and diagnosis of persons
with fetal alcohol syndrome.

![Reducing Alcohol-Exposed Pregnancies: A Report of the National Task Force on
Fetal Alcohol Syndrome and Fetal Alcohol Effect](/ncbddd/fasd/images/Reducing-
Alcohol-Exposed-Pregnancies-135px.jpg)

Reducing Alcohol-Exposed Pregnancies: A Report of the National Task Force on
Fetal Alcohol Syndrome and Fetal Alcohol Effect pdf icon[6 MB, 36 Pages, 508]
pdf icon[Accessible version] [205 KB, 31 Pages, Print Only]  
This report reviews current evidence on prevention strategies to reduce
alcohol use and alcohol-exposed pregnancies, provides recommendations on
promoting and improving these strategies, and offers future research
directions in the field of FASD prevention.

![Call to Action Cover](/ncbddd/fasd/images/08_121973CallActionCov-sm.jpg)

A Call to Action: Advancing Essential Services and Research on Fetal Alcohol
Spectrum Disorders - A Report of the National Task Force on Fetal Alcohol
Syndrome and Fetal Alcohol Effect pdf icon[4 MB, 28 Pages, Print Only] pdf
icon[Accessible version] [274 KB, 31 Pages, Print Only]  
This report highlights recommendations to improve and expand efforts regarding
early identification, diagnostic services, and quality research on
interventions for individuals with FASDs and their families.

![U.S. Surgeon General’s Advisory on Alcohol Use in Pregnancy
](/ncbddd/fasd/images/sg-advisory-on-fas-cdc-135px.jpg)

U.S. Surgeon General's Advisory on Alcohol Use in Pregnancy pdf icon[223 KB, 2
Pages, Print Only]  
This double-sided card contains the 2005 U.S. Surgeon General's Advisory on
Alcohol Use in Pregnancy on one side and Internet resources about alcohol use
and pregnancy on the reverse side.
