### **Office of the Associate Director for Communication (OADC)**

From the Director

![Associate Director for
Communication](/about/images/leadership/leaders/adc.jpg)

The Office of the Associate Director for Communication (OADC) supports CDC's
24/7 work to protect America's safety, health, and security, whether they
begin at home or abroad. Our office pdf icon[PDF - 122 KB] is driven by CDC's
communication principles pdf icon[831 KB, 1 Page, HTML] and the mission and
organizational goals listed below. I invite you to learn more about OADC, our
success measures, and our numerous communication channels.

-Michelle E. Bonds, MBA  
Acting Associate Director for Communication, CDC

### **OADC 's Mission**

Leading customer-centered, science-based, and high-impact communication.

### **OADC 's Organizational Goals**

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Maximize strategic communication across CDC.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Ensure CDC's work is accessible, understandable, and actionable.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Maximize public trust in and credibility of CDC's science, programs, and recommendations.
