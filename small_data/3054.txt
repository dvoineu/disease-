Basic Information

![Photo of three women](/cancer/ovarian/images/3women-300.jpg)

Learn how to lower your risk and about the symptoms, risk factors, and
treatment for ovarian cancer.

Statistics

![Photo of a person looking at statistics about the United States on a digital
tablet](/cancer/prostate/images/usmap-tablet-300x200.jpg)

Ovarian cancer causes more deaths each year than any other gynecologic cancer
in the U.S. The Data Visualizations Tool provides detailed statistics.

_Inside Knowledge_

![Photo of a woman](/cancer/gynecologic/images/ik-300x200.jpg)

CDC's _Inside Knowledge_ campaign raises awareness of the five main types of
gynecologic cancer: cervical, ovarian, uterine, vaginal, and vulvar.

Knowledge Is Power: Ovarian Cancer

Knowledge is Power: Ovarian Cancer

pdf iconView Transcript media iconLow Resolution Video

This short animated video informs women that ovarian cancer has warning signs;
know the symptoms, listen to your body, and see a doctor if you notice
anything unusual.
