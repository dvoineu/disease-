# Let's Talk About Sexual Health

Minus

Related Pages

Talking about sex may not be a regular part of your doctor-patient
relationship, but it should be. This can be especially true for adolescents
and young adults who are disproportionately impacted by sexually transmitted
diseases (STDs). Half of all new STD cases are in young people between the
ages of 15 and 24. This video discusses the importance of a healthy dialogue
between youth and providers concerning their sexual health, and features CDC
Epidemiologist, Elizabeth Torrone, PhD MSPH. (November 30, 2012)
