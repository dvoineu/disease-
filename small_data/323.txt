![Effects of Drinking Alcohol on Your Health](/alcoholportal/images/alcohol-
portal-banner.png)

While most people know that drinking too much alcohol can lead to injuries and
deaths in car crashes, many people do not know that drinking too much alcohol
also can increase the chances of  cancer, suicide, unintended pregnancies,
sexually transmitted infections, and other negative health outcomes. CDC
provides for the common defense of the country and, as such, has a significant
role in fighting excessive alcohol use through the use of our science,
tracking, and service to the countless Americans and their families affected
by drinking too much.
