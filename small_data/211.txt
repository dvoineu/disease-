STARI

Where lone star ticks can be found...

Learn more

A rash similar to the rash of Lyme disease has been described in humans
following bites of the lone star tick, _Amblyomma americanum_. The rash may be
accompanied by fatigue, fever, headache, muscle and joint pains. This
condition has been named southern tick-associated rash illness (STARI). The
cause of STARI is not known.
