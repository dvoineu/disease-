# Human Papillomavirus (HPV)

Minus

Related Pages

![Smiling, happy family](/hpv/images/home-smiling-family.jpg)

HPV is a common virus that can lead to certain types of cancer later in life.
Getting your 11-12 year-old child two doses of the HPV vaccine can prevent
these cancers.
