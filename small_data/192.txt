Living with Spina Bifida

CDC programs and research improve quality of life and encourage full
participation at every age for those living with spina bifida.

Learn more

Spina bifida is a major birth defect of a person's spine. With good quality
medical care people with spina bifida can reach their full potential. If you
have spina bifida, or know someone who does, it's important to get the facts
so that you can make the best possible health care choices.
