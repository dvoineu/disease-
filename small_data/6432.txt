## Meetings and Agendas

The 15-member BSC generally convenes twice a year in Washington, DC. The
workgroups of the BSC meet more frequently during the year usually by
teleconference. Past meeting minutes and agendas are available for public
review.

Materials presented and distributed during BSC meetings can be viewed on NIOSH
Docket 278.

For information prior to 2013, please contact the BSC.
