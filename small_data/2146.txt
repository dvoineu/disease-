### Division of Injury Prevention

![Judy Qualters](/injury/images/about/JudyQualters_110x140.png)



**Judith R. Qualters, PhD, MPH  **  
**Director**  
Division topic areas include: Core State Violence and Injury Prevention
Program, Injury Control Research Center Program, Web-based Injury Statistics
Query and Reporting System _(WISQARS),_ Older Adult Fall Prevention,
Transportation Safety, Traumatic Brain Injury Prevention, Suicide Prevention,
Injury Prevention in American Indian and Alaskan Native Communities,
Statistics, Economics, Programming, and Data Science.

### Division of Overdose Prevention

![Grant Baldwin](/injury/images/about/GrantBaldwin_110x140.png)

**Grant Baldwin, PhD, MPH**

**Director**

Division topic areas include Opioid Overdose, Prevention for States, Cannabis
Strategy and Coordination, and Health Systems and Research.

### Division of Violence Prevention

![Jim Mercy](/injury/images/about/JimMercy2015_110x140.png)

**James A. Mercy, PhD**

**Director**

Division topic areas include Child Maltreatment, Intimate Partner Violence,
Sexual Violence, Teen Dating Violence, and Youth Violence.
