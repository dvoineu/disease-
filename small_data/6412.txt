# Diversity in NIOSH

Minus

Related Pages

![Diversity Logo](/niosh/images/logo.gif)**MISSION:** To enhance the
organization's ability to attract, recruit, hire, develop, retain, and serve a
diverse population by fostering an inclusive environment that embraces, values
and respects all individuals.

![Square box of people](/niosh/images/squareboxpeople.jpg)**VALUE:** By
focusing on diversity enhancement, NIOSH will become an organization that
encourages, values and effectively utilizes the diverse talents, skills and
abilities of its workforce to provide world-class products and services to the
public.

**ACTION:** The Institute is committed to a workplace in which all people are
respected as individuals and are valued for their contributions to
accomplishing its mission.

For further information, please contact:  
Casey Chosewood, Champion, Diversity Steering Council  
Constance Franklin, Diversity Steering Council Chair
