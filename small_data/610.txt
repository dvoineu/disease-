Congressional Justifications

When the President submits the Executive Branch Budget to Congress each year,
the Executive agencies submit a document called the Congressional
Justification.

Learn more

Operating Plans

Once Congress passes an annual appropriations bill, agencies develop an
operating plan based on the bill and conference report.

Learn more

Budget Fact Sheets

The CDC budget fact sheets highlight CDC's priorities in the President's
Budget request. They also provide an overview of each account's mission,
activities, and performance highlights.
