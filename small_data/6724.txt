# State, Local, and Tribal Heart Disease and Stroke Programs

Minus

Related Pages

CDC's Division for Heart Disease and Stroke Prevention (DHDSP) supports state,
local, and tribal efforts to prevent, manage, and reduce the risk factors
associated with heart disease and stroke. DHDSP, along with the Division of
Diabetes Translation also supports all 50 states and the District of Columbia
in their efforts to address the serious national health problems of diabetes,
heart disease, and stroke.
