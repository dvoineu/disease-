####  **January 2020**

New data that are available in OSHData:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Medicaid Cessation Coverage, from American Lung Association, 2019 (4th Quarter).
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        * Legislative data on E-Cigarettes, Fire Safety, Licensure, Preemption, Smokefree Campus, Smokefree Indoor Air, Tax and Youth Access Legislation from Office on Smoking and Health (OSH), 2019 (4th Quarter).
