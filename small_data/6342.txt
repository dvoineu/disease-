CDC develops materials designed to teach health professionals, policy makers,
the media, and the public about cancer prevention and control. They are
available online as web pages, Adobe® Acrobat® PDF documents, or both. We
offer a variety of fact sheets, brochures, posters, and more, including a
selection in Spanish.

You can order printed copies of some of our publications free of charge.
Quantities are limited and this service is available only in the United
States.
