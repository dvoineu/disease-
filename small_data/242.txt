# Immunization-related News

Minus

Related Pages

CDC's National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases distributes a
variety of immunology- and vaccine-related publications and other
scientifically reviewed resources available to a range of audiences, among
them researchers, physicians, media, and the general public. These include
updates, reports, statistics, graphics, media kits, and comprehensive details
of each vaccine-preventable disease and its recommended vaccine.

For your convenience, we also provide links to news and other materials
produced by some of our partners.
