# Parasites - _Balamuthia mandrillaris_ \- Granulomatous Amebic Encephalitis
(GAE)

Minus

Related Pages

![Balamuthia mandrillaris image](/parasites/balamuthia/images/balamuthia-
homepage-image.jpg)

_Balamuthia mandrillaris_ is a free-living ameba (a single-celled living
organism) naturally found in the environment. _Balamuthia_ can cause a rare*
and serious infection of the brain called granulomatous amebic encephalitis
(GAE).
