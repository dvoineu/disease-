# Parasites - Fasciolopsiasis (Fasciolopsis infection)

Minus

Related Pages



![intestinal fluke Fasciolopsis
buski](/parasites/images/fasciolopsis/home_page_image_fasciolopsis.jpg)The
intestinal fluke _Fasciolopsis buski_ , which causes faciolopsiasis, is the
largest intestinal fluke of humans. Fasciolopsiasis can be prevented by
cooking aquatic plants well before eating them. _Fasciolopsis_ is found in
south and southeastern Asia. Fasciolopsiasis is treatable.

Image: Left: _Fasciolopsis buski_ egg in an unstained wet mount. Center: Adult
fluke of _F. buski_ next to a scale. Right: Snail in the genus _Hippeutis_ ,
an intermediate host for _F. buski_. Credit: DPDx, Image courtesy of
Conchology, Inc, Mactan Island, Philippines.
