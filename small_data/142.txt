# Human Papillomavirus (HPV)

Minus

Related Pages

![HPV vaccine can prevent some health effects caused by the
virus.](/std/hpv/images/hpv-4-sme.jpg)

The HPV vaccine can prevent genital warts and some cancers.

Learn more about who should be vaccinated.

Fact Sheet

![father mother son and daughter](/std/hpv/images/WHT-MO-DAU-SON-FA-1.jpg)

There are many different types of HPV. All boys and girls ages 11 or 12 years
should get vaccinated.

More

HPV & Men Fact Sheet

![four men](/std/hpv/images/Diverse-Men-4.jpg)

HPV can cause men to develop genital warts, or some kinds of cancer.
