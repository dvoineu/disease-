# Funding Profiles

Minus

Related Pages

The CDC Funding Data and Profiles site offers quick access to information on
CDC funding provided to recipients in states and territories to address issues
of public health importance. This includes funding provided to state and local
health departments, universities, and other public and private agencies for a
variety of public health programs. The CDC Grant Funding Profiles site has
state-and-territory-specific grants funding profiles starting with fiscal year
2010.
