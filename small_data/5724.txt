**CDC-INFO**

Have a question about malaria or other parasitic diseases? Need a publication?
CDC-INFO is a consolidated response center that provides general information
and publications about a wide variety of health topics to the general public
via telephone or e-mail.

Contact CDC-INFO:

Via telephone: 1-800-CDC-INFO (1-800-232-4636) / TTY: 1-888-232-6348  
Via the web: Contact CDC-INFO

Hours: 8am-8pm ET / Monday-Friday (closed on weekends and holidays)

call one of the hotlines below. Guidance for diagnosis and treatment of

providers only; healthcare providers should not direct patients to these
hotlines for information._
