# Epilepsy Reports

Minus

Related Pages

Researchers, healthcare providers, and public health professionals can learn
more about epilepsy through these reports developed by US and International
health organizations. The reports address such topics as the definition of
epilepsy, treatment, public health impacts, goals, and recommendations.

                      * School Health Profiles and Practices Brief: Epilepsy and Seizure Disorder Cdc-pdf[PDF - 2 MB]  
This brief highlights epilepsy-related data from the 2014 School Health
Profiles survey and shares resources for schools, parents, and communities.

                      * International League Against Epilepsy (ILAE): A Practical Clinical Definition of EpilepsyExternal  
This 2014 report provides the ILAE's recent guidelines for diagnosing
epilepsy.

                      * Prevention Research Centers' Managing Epilepsy Well Network Cdc-pdf[PDF - 5 MB]  
The MEW Network is one of CDC's Prevention Research Centers Program thematic
networks.

                      * Institute of Medicine Report: Epilepsy Across the SpectrumExternal  
In this report, the Institute of Medicine (IOM) looks at the public health
dimensions of epilepsy and makes wide-ranging recommendations to improve the
lives of people with epilepsy and their families.

                      * Living with Epilepsy: A Blueprint for the Future. Task Force Update Cdc-pdf[PDF-782K]  
This 2009 report from the Living with Epilepsy Task Force updates their goals
and objectives for improving the lives of people with epilepsy and their
families.

                      * Neurological Disorders: Public Health ChallengesExternal  
This 2007 World Health Organization (WHO) Report addresses important public
health aspects of epilepsy and other neurological disorders.

                      * Epilepsy in North AmericaExternal  
This 2006 regional report is a modified and shortened version of the Global
Campaign Against Epilepsy report. It describes the public health issues of
epilepsy in North America and the need for a regional plan to address these
issues.

                      * Living Well with Epilepsy II Cdc-pdf[PDF-976K]  
This Report of the 2003 National Conference on Public Health and Epilepsy
provides recommendations for the public health agenda on epilepsy.

                      * Living Well with Epilepsy - Cdc-pdf[PDF-371K]  
This is a report on the 1997 National Conference on Public Health and
Epilepsy.

                      * Global Campaign Against Epilepsy: Out of the ShadowsExternal  
This 1997 report was sponsored by the World Health Organization, International
League Against Epilepsy and the International Bureau for Epilepsy. The
campaign's mission is to improve acceptability, treatment, services, and
prevention of epilepsy worldwide.
