# The Many Faces of Stroke: Stroke Survivor Stories

Minus

Related Pages

Stroke, the fifth leading cause of death in the United States, can happen to
anyone at any time. Just ask these survivors. From undiagnosed high blood
pressure to unknown family history for stroke, many things can affect stroke
risk. Read these survivor stories to learn more about stroke, including how
you may be at risk, how to recognize stroke, and what to do if stroke happens.

### Featured Stories

Adrian Cushenberry

![Adrian Cushenberry and her family.](/stroke/images/adrian-cushenberry-
family.jpg)

Active, healthy, and just 49 years old, Adrian thought he was the last person
who needed to think about stroke. But Adrian did not realize that he had an
increased risk of stroke due to family history. Read Adrian's story.

Alyson Mccord

![Alyson McCord](/stroke/images/alyson-mccord.jpg)

A series of mini-strokes, followed by a major stroke, compelled Alyson to get
her high blood pressure under control and make other lifestyle changes. Read
Alyson's story.

Brooke Bergfeld

![Brooke Bergfeld and his family.](/stroke/images/brooke-bergfeld-family.jpg)

Brooke had a stroke just one week after giving birth at age 29. She did not
realize that women are at risk for stroke during and after childbirth. Read
Brooke's story.

Clark Caffall

![Clark Caffall](/stroke/images/clark-caffall.jpg)

Clark Caffall always ate a healthy diet, but his high cholesterol may have
been inherited. He urges people to get regular checkups and to have their
cholesterol levels checked. Read Clark's story.

John Murphy

![John Murphy](/stroke/images/john-murphy.jpg)

John struggled for years with unhealthy weight, but he didn't realize how his
lifestyle, including his food choices, affected his stroke risk. John had a
stroke at age 45. Read John's story.

Lilian Tsi Stielstra

![Lilian Tsi Stielstra](/stroke/images/lilian-tsi-stielstra.jpg)

Lilian didn't realize that getting more physical activity could help lower her
high blood pressure, a major risk for stroke. Her stroke at age 46 was a wake-
up call to change her eating and activity habits. Read Lilian's story.

Prince Quire

![Prince Quire](/stroke/images/prince-quire-headshot.jpg)

Prince didn't know that he had uncontrolled high blood pressure--a leading
risk factor for stroke. He had a stroke when he was just 39. Read Prince's
story.

Wanda Sturtz

![Wanda Sturtz](/stroke/images/wanda-sturz.jpg)

Wanda Sturtz tells her story to encourage others to make changes that can
minimize their risk of stroke. Read Wanda's story.
