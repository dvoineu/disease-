###

Learn about our contributions to the Global Health Security Agenda.

Global health protection is about more than one country, one issue, or one
pathogen. Many factors affect our health security, like infectious diseases,
humanitarian crises, and the growing burden of noncommunicable diseases. No
matter what the cause, the Division of Global Health Protection (DGHP) and its
partners across CDC and the globe are working to protect health and save
lives.
