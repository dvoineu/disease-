COVID-19 Update: Optimization Strategies for Healthcare Personal Protective
Equipment (PPE)

media iconLow Resolution Video

#### **Overview**

During this COCA Call, presenters will provide a COVID-19 update and discuss
strategies for healthcare facilities to optimize personal protective equipment
(PPE) supplies such as eye protection, isolation gowns, facemasks, and N95
respirators.
