CDC experts and invited bloggers provide blog posts on topics in genomics and
precision health that are important to health care providers, public health
professionals, policy makers and the public. This page provides links to each
blog post and will be updated on a regular basis. We also link to related blog
posts from other CDC programs. Please visit our Genomics and Precision Health
blog post home page.
