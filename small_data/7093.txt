# Air Quality

Minus

Related Pages

Outdoor air quality has improved since the 1990s, but many challenges remain
in protecting Americans from air quality problems. Ground-level ozone, the
main part of smog, and particle pollution are just two of the many threats to
air quality and public health in the United States.

Air Quality Flag Program

![image of the Air Quality Flag Program infographic](/air/images/air-quality-
infographic-small.JPG)

The **Air Quality Flag Program** alerts school communities to local air
quality forecasts so they can take actions to protect children, especially
those with asthma.
