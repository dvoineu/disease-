More

The Office of Financial Resources (OFR) serves CDC/ATSDR and the public by
providing leadership for the agency's acquisition, budgetary, grants and
accounting initiatives. In addition, OFR ensures that CDC accomplishes its
public health mission through agency-wide fiscal accountability and oversight,
and supports CDC's public health mission to "save money through prevention" by
ensuring appropriate fiscal stewardship of the tax payer dollar.
