# Fetal Alcohol Spectrum Disorders (FASDs)

FASD en EspaÃ±ol Home

Related Pages

Fetal alcohol spectrum disorders (FASDs) are a group of conditions that can
occur in a person whose mother drank alcohol during pregnancy. FASDs are
preventable if a woman does not drink alcohol during pregnancy.

commitment to reduce or stop drinking and/or use contraception effectively.
