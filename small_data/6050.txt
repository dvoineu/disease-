# Real Stories: Living with Spina Bifida

Minus

Related Pages

Spina bifida is a condition that affects the spine and is usually apparent at
birth. It is a type of neural tube defect (NTD). To learn what it's like to
live with this condition or how families are affected, read these real stories
from people living with spina bifida.
