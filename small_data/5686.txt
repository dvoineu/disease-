## American Heart Month 2020: High Blood Pressure Control--We've Got This!

Uncontrolled high blood pressure, or hypertension, is dangerous and far too
common. In fact, 1 of 3 adults in the United States has the condition.1 High
blood pressure usually has no signs or symptoms, but it does have
consequences. The only way to know if you're at risk for high blood pressure
is to know your numbers.

This year, the Division for Heart Disease and Stroke Prevention (DHDSP) will
focus on how people can control high blood pressure and protect their heart.
Join DHDSP throughout February in encouraging people with high blood pressure
on their journey to control. We've got this!
