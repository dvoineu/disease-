Basic Information

![Photo of two women](/cancer/breast/images/2women-300.jpg)

Learn what breast cancer is and about symptoms, risk factors, mammograms,
diagnosis, and treatment for breast cancer.

Statistics

![Photo of a person looking at statistics about the United States on a digital
tablet](/cancer/prostate/images/usmap-tablet-300x200.jpg)

Breast cancer is the second most common cancer among women in the United
States. The Data Visualizations Tool provides detailed statistics.

Low-Cost Screening

![Photo of a woman getting a
mammogram](/spanish/cancer/breast/images/mammogram-300x200.jpg)

CDC's National Breast and Cervical Cancer Early Detection Program provides
low-cost breast and cervical cancer screenings. Learn more.

Breast Cancer Treatment Plan

Featured Resources

![Photo of Charity, a breast cancer

CDC's _Bring Your Brave_ campaign educates younger women about breast cancer.

![Photo of a woman getting a
mammogram](/cancer/breast/images/mammogram-160.jpg)

Mammograms are the best test to find breast cancer early. Learn more.

![Photo of a woman](/cancer/breast/images/woman-160x120.jpg)

Learn what triple-negative breast cancer is and how it's treated.

CDC works with partners to reduce the burden of breast cancer in the United
