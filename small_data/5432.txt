# Canine Influenza (Dog Flu)

EspaÃ±ol

![Influenza in Dogs](/flu/images/other/influenza-in-dogs-tp4.jpg)

Canine influenza (also known as dog flu) is a contagious respiratory disease
in dogs caused by specific Type A influenza viruses known to infect dogs.
These are called "canine influenza viruses." Dog flu is a disease of dogs. No
human infections with canine influenza have ever been reported. There are two
different influenza A dog flu viruses: one is an H3N8 virus and the other is
an H3N2 virus.
