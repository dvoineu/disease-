# Food Safety Features

Minus

Related Pages

**These CDC feature stories give you information you need to protect yourself
and your families from illnesses caused by germs in food and water.**

You can add any or all of these features to your website. CDC's content
syndication program provides free, credible health content that's easy to add
to websites. Visit our Public Health Media Library to learn more about content
syndication. To find a feature, search for it using its title.

Foods and Germs

![antibiotic resistance, food, and food producing
animals](/ncezid/dfwed/images/antibiotic-resistance-food_86px.jpg)

**Antibiotic Resistance, Food, and Food-Producing Animals  
**More than 400,000 Americans get sick every year from infections caused by
antibiotic-resistant foodborne bacteria, according to CDC estimates.

![chicken and knife on cutting
board](/ncezid/dfwed/images/salmonellachicken_86px.jpg)

**Chicken and Food Poisoning (English | Español)  
**Americans eat more chicken every year than any other meat. Chicken can be a
nutritious choice, but raw chicken is often contaminated with  _Campylobacter_
bacteria and sometimes with _Salmonella_  and  _Clostridium perfringens_
bacteria.

![eating out safely](/ncezid/dfwed/images/food-dining-safety_86px.jpg)

**Food Safety and Dining Out (English | Español)  
**When choosing a restaurant, look for one that keeps food safety on the menu.

![globe with fork next to it](/ncezid/dfwed/images/food-globe-86px.jpg)

**Food Safety: A Changing Landscape in a Global World**  
Global food safety is important for the health of all Americans. Today, the
United States imports about 15% of its food supply from more than 200
countries or territories.

![Food safety smart graphic](/ncezid/dfwed/images/food-safety-savvy_86px.png)

**Food Safety in the Kitchen**  
Use these tools and tips to help prevent food poisoning.

![fruit and vegetable safety](/ncezid/dfwed/images/vegetables-86px.jpg)

**Fruit and Vegetable Safety (English | Español)**  
Eating a diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables provides important health
benefits when you select and prepare them safely.

![global networks and safe food](/ncezid/dfwed/images/lady-selling-
fruit-86px.jpg)

**Global Networks Make Food Safer**  
Foodborne diseases are preventable, yet they commonly cause illness,
disability, and death worldwide. Find out how CDC and global partners are
providing countries with tools and training to make food safer to eat.

![Home Canning](/ncezid/dfwed/images/homecanning_86px.jpg)

**Home Canning and Botulism  (English** |  **Español)**  
Home canning is an excellent way to preserve garden produce and share it with
family and friends, but it can be risky or even deadly if not done correctly
and safely.

![A plate of pot roast with
vegetables.](/ncezid/dfwed/images/potroast_86px.jpg)

**Key Facts About Food Poisoning (English | Español)**  
Learn the facts about food poisoning: tips to prevent it, who is more likely
to get it, and when to see a doctor if you have symptoms.

![vibrio and raw oysters](/ncezid/dfwed/images/vibrio-raw-oysters_85px.jpg)

**Oysters and Vibriosis  (English | Español** **)**  
Raw oysters can ruin your summer. That's because you can get very sick from
eating raw oysters. Learn about vibriosis, a disease linked to raw oysters

![Different age groups gathered around table](/ncezid/dfwed/images/food-
safety-groups_86px.jpg)

**Prevent Illness from _Clostridium Perfringens_ (English | Español)**  
_Clostridium perfringens (C. perfringens)_ is one of the most common causes of
food poisoning in the United States. Learn more about how to prevent illness
from this germ.

![Washing your hands with soap and
water](/ncezid/dfwed/images/norovirus_a86px.jpg)

**Prevent the Spread of Norovirus **  **(English** |  **Español)**  
Norovirus causes many people to become ill with vomiting and diarrhea each
year. You can help protect yourself and others by washing your hands often and
following simple tips to stay healthy.

![Drinking glass of milk](/ncezid/dfwed/images/rawmilk_86px.jpg)

**Raw (Unpasteurized) Milk ** **(English | Español)**  
Raw milk can carry harmful germs that can make you very sick or kill you. If
you're thinking about drinking raw milk because you believe it has health
benefits, consider other options.

![Dad and daughter eating food](/ncezid/dfwed/images/salmonella-food_86px.jpg)

**_Salmonella_ and Eggs**  **(English | Español)**  
Eggs are one of nature's most nutritious and economical foods. However, they
also carry a significant risk of carrying _Salmonella_ if not handled or
cooked correctly. Learn how to reduce the risks of a _Salmonella_ infection
from eggs.

![Image of family making cookies](/ncezid/dfwed/images/no-raw-dough_86px.png)

**_Salmonella_ and Food** ( **English** | **Español**)  
You may know that _Salmonella_ can contaminate poultry and eggs but it also
sneaks its way into many other foods - ground beef, tuna, pork, tomatoes,
sprouts, and even peanut butter. Learn what you can do to make your food safer
to eat.

![salmonella and eggs](/ncezid/dfwed/images/eggs-86px.jpg)

**Say No to Raw Dough! (English | Español)**  
For many people, the holiday season is the perfect time to spend time together
in the kitchen and share delicious baked foods and desserts. Follow these
safety tips to help you and your loved ones stay healthy when handling raw
dough.

![mom dad and child](/ncezid/dfwed/images/ecoli-86px.jpg)

**Shiga Toxin-Producing _E.coli_ and Food Safety**  **(English | Español)**  
Although most kinds of _E. coli_ are harmless, others can make you sick. Learn
about _E. coli_ and what you can do to help lower the risk of infection.

![woman looking at food in delivery meal kit box](/ncezid/dfwed/images/food-
delivery-safety_86px.png)

**Tips for Meal Kit and Food Delivery Safety  
**Subscription meal kits, mail-order food, and home-delivered groceries offer
convenience.  Make sure food safety is part of the package, too.

Seasons, Holidays, and Events

![Child riding horse on mary-go-round](/ncezid/dfwed/images/child-
carnival-86px.jpg)

**Food Safety at Fairs and Festivals (English | Español)**  
A fun summer activity is attending fairs, festivals, carnivals, and rodeos.
Follow these tips to have safe cooking, eating, and drinking experiences at
those events.

![Image of Cooked Turkey](/ncezid/dfwed/images/turkey-355px.jpg)

**Food Safety Tips for Your Holiday Turkey (English | Español)**  
Food handling errors and inadequate cooking are the most common problems that
lead to poultry-associated foodborne disease outbreaks in the United States.
Follow these four food safety tips to help you safely prepare your next
holiday turkey meal.

![plate of food](/foodsafety/images/holidayfoodsafety_86px.jpg)

**Food Safety Tips for the Holidays**  
Follow these simple tips to keep safe from food poisoning, or foodborne
illness, during the holidays.

![Superbowl party food
spread](/ncezid/dfwed/images/superbowlplaybook_86px.jpg)

**Game Day Food Safety Tips (English | Español)**  
Tackling a buffet at your Super Bowl gathering? Practice these game rules and
keep the runs on the field.

![Picutre of chitlins](/ncezid/dfwed/images/preparing-chitlins-
safely_86px.png)

**Preparing Chitlins Safely**  
Chitlins are a traditional Southern food served during winter holidays. If
you're getting ready to cook chitlins, sometimes called chitterlings, it's
important to handle and cook these pork intestines the right way.

Pregnant Women and Infants

![dad feeding infant](/ncezid/dfwed/images/dad-feeding-infant-86px.jpg)

**Learn About _Cronobacter_ Infection (English | Spanish)  
**_Cronobacter_ is a germ found naturally in the environment that can survive
in very dry conditions. _Cronobacter_ has been found in dry foods, such as
powdered infant formula, powdered milk, herbal teas, and starches.

![Close up of a pregnant woman with hands on her
stomach.](/ncezid/dfwed/images/pregnant_86px.jpg)

**Prevent Infections During Pregnancy (English | Spanish)  
**If you're pregnant or planning a pregnancy, there are simple steps you can
take to protect your fetus or newborn from infections that cause serious
health problems.
