Global Health

![Global Health and You
logo](http://www.cdc.gov/globalhealth/images/gh_and_you/globalhealthandyou.jpg)

CDC leverages its core strengths to advance four overarching global health
goals: 1) improving the health and well-being of people around the world, 2)
improving capabilities for preparing for and responding to infectious diseases
and emerging health threats, 3) building country public health capacity, and
4) maximizing organizational capacity. (To learn more about these goals and
our vision, see CDC's Global Health Strategy.)
