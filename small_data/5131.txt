CDC-Trained Disease Detective Mobilizes her Community Against Dengue

* * *

In 2019 Honduras faced the largest recorded dengue outbreak in its history.
The largest department, Olancho, jumped from 70 reported cases in 2018, to
more than 600 in 2019. After eleven children under the age of fifteen died
from dengue, Dr. Ofelia Martinez of the Olancho Health Department was
determined to make a change.
