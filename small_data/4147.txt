# Employment

Minus

Related Pages

Whether you are looking for full time employment, internships, or positions
overseas, these links will guide you to the information you need.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * CDC employment  
Learn about career fields, benefits, new hire information, and working at CDC.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * CDC's Recruitment Events  
Use CDC's tool to learn about a variety of recruitment and outreach events to
attract and connect with talented individuals interested in available career
opportunities.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    * Opportunities for Students and College Graduates  
Find a listing of different opportunities available at CDC and ATSDR and a
'program matrix' which speeds process of identifying good matches for your
education, experience, interests, and goals.

Qualified applicants are encouraged to apply through the CDC web site at
https://jobs.cdc.gov

CDC jobs can also be located at www.usajobs.govExternal which lists all
Department of Health and Human Services positions.
