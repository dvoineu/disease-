# Program Performance One-Pagers

Minus

Related Pages

Program Performance One-Pagers (PPOPs) describe the relevance and impact of
NIOSH programs in a short and easy to understand format. Each PPOP is intended
to highlight the following areas:

**What are our priorities?** - Outlines the program's occupational safety and
health priorities  
**What do we do?** - Describes the program's major activities in these
priority areas  
**What have we accomplished?** - Highlights the program's accomplishments from
the past year  
**What 's next?** - Notes planned work for the upcoming year

In addition, NIOSH and some of our mandatory programs are also highlighted in
this easy to understand format.
