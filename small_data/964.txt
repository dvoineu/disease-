# Information for Healthcare Professionals

Minus

Related Pages

Interim Guidance for Healthcare Professionals

CDC interim guidance for evaluating patients, close contacts and clusters,
reporting persons under investigation (PUIs), testing specimens, and infection
control.

medical icon

Clinical Features

CDC interim information for clinicians on human infections with MERS-CoV.

badge icon

Preparedness

Checklists and resources to help healthcare providers and facilities enhance
preparedness for MERS-CoV infection control.

cleaning icon

Infection Prevention and Control

Interim recommendations for managing hospitalized patients with known or
suspected MERS-CoV infection.

search icon

Monitoring and Movement Guidance

CDC Interim U.S. guidance for monitoring people potentially exposed to MERS-
CoV and evaluating their intended travel, including the application of
movement restrictions when indicated.

home icon

Home Care and Isolation or Quarantine

CDC interim guidance on implementing home care and isolation or quarantine of
people confirmed to have, or being evaluated for, MERS.

plane solid icon

Guidance on Air Medical Transport (AMT)

Updated guidance to assist AMT service providers transporting MERS patients.
