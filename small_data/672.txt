Epilepsy is a broad term used for a brain disorder that causes seizures. There
are many different types of epilepsy. There are also different kinds of
seizures.

Seizure First Aid

Learn how to take care of someone during a seizure.

Types of Seizures

Learn about the different types of seizures.

Fast Facts

Find out about epilepsy in the United States, such as how many people have
epilepsy.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Read the answers to common questions about epilepsy.

Sudden Unexpected Death in Epilepsy (SUDEP)

Learn about this rare cause of death in people with epilepsy.
