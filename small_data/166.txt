# Typhoid Fever and Paratyphoid Fever

Minus

Related Pages

![Asain travelers in airport](/typhoid-fever/images/iStock_000085685315-asain-
travelers.jpg)

Typhoid fever and paratyphoid fever are life-threatening illnesses caused by
_Salmonella_ serotype Typhi and _Salmonella_ serotype Paratyphi, respectively.

Most people in the United States with typhoid fever or paratyphoid fever
become infected while traveling abroad, most often to countries where these
diseases are common. If you plan to travel outside of the United States, find
out if you need the typhoid fever vaccine at _www.cdc.gov/travel_.

Travel Alert

There is an ongoing outbreak of extensively drug-resistant typhoid fever in
Pakistan. Travelers to Pakistan and other South Asian countries should take
precautions:

                    * Read the alert
                    * Get a typhoid fever vaccination
                    * Follow safe food and water guidelines
