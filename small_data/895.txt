# Determine Your Funding Eligibility

Minus

Related Pages

Contracts Funding Eligibility

CDC is committed to helping small businesses grow and prosper and provides
contracting opportunity for small businesses. In order to bid on and win
government small business set-asides, a business must qualify as "small"
according to the SBA's industry size standards.

For more information on determining **Small Business** funding eligibility,
visit CDC's contracts site.

Grants Funding Eligibility

CDC funds research and non-research projects via grants and cooperative
agreements (awards). The eligibility criteria for these awards is found in
CDC's Funding Opportunity Announcements (FOAs) listed on
www.grants.govExternal. Each FOA outlines who is eligible to apply, special
eligibility requirements, and the justification for eligibility.

For more information on determining grants funding eligibility, visit CDC's
grants site.
