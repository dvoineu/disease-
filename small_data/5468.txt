# Hereditary Breast and Ovarian Cancer

Minus

Related Pages

If you are a woman with a family health history of breast or ovarian cancer,
you may be more likely to get these cancers yourself. Collecting your family
health history and sharing this information with your doctor can help you find
out if you're at higher risk. If so, you can take steps to lower your risk.
