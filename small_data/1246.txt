Venous thromboembolism (VTE), also known as blood clots, is an underdiagnosed
and serious, but preventable medical condition. It is important to know about
VTE because it can happen to anybody at any age and cause serious illness,
disability, and in some cases, death. The good news is that VTE can be
prevented and treated if discovered early.
