### Materials from Other Organizations

![Graphic: Articles available from the National
Center](/ncbddd/adhd/images/res-ctr-factsheets-sm.jpg)

The National Resource Center (NRC) on ADHDexternal icon  
The NRC is funded by the CDC to provide information and educational materials
on this disorder. They have developed "What We Know" sheets to keep the
general public and professionals up to date on new developments in research,
treatments and successful strategies for children and adults with ADHD.

![Photo: Cover of brochure](/ncbddd/adhd/images/nimh_adhd_cover.jpg)

The National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) Bookletsexternal icon  
Information on ADHD including an easy-to-read booklet and a detailed booklet
that describes ADHD symptoms, causes, and treatments, with information on
getting help and coping.
