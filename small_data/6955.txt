# Funded State Education and Health Agencies

Minus

Related Pages

The following state education and health agencies are funded by CDC's Division
of Adolescent and School Health to conduct school-based surveillance on youth
risk behaviors and school health policies and practices.

![map of the continental United States displaying states, territories funded
for School-Based
Surveillance](/healthyyouth/images/maps/1807_surveillance_funding_map.jpg)
