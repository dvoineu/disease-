# Information for Parents with Teens (Ages 12-19)

Minus

Related Pages

![Graphic showing several teens.](/parents/teens/images/02_teen.jpg)

As a parent of a teen, you are beginning to see your child become an adult.
These are critical years that will affect both their current and future
health. These links will help you learn how to guide your teen to become a
healthy and productive adult.
