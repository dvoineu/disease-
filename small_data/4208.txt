# 2014-2016 Ebola Outbreak Distribution in West Africa

Minus

Related Pages

**The 2014-2016 Ebola outbreak in West Africa has ended. Visit theEbola
Outbreak section for information on current Ebola outbreaks.**

The largest Ebola outbreak in history was first reported in March 2014 and
declared over by the World Health Organization (WHO) on June 10, 2016. While
the epidemic spread to other parts of Africa, Europe, and the United States,
the largest impact was in Guinea, Sierra Leone, and Liberia, the epicenter of
the outbreak. Over the duration of this epidemic, there were 28,616 suspected,
probable, and confirmed cases from these three countries and 11,310 deaths.

The following tables illustrates the number of confirmed cases found by
prefecture in Guinea, Sierra Leone, and Liberia as of March 27, 2016, when WHO
terminated the Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC) for
the Ebola outbreak in West Africa.
