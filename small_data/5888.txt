# Zika Communication Toolkits

Minus

Related Pages

These toolkits contain communication materials that can be tailored for
various groups to use when preparing for local transmission of Zika virus in
the United States.

Begin your toolkit with the Basics, then add an additional kit tailored for
your group's needs. Can't find your group? More information for specific
groups is available.
