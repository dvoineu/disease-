#### Flu Can Be Very Serious - Millions Get Sick, 30-Second Spot

Flu can be very serious. In the U.S., millions of people get sick every year.
People risk missing school, time with family and friends and important life
events. Everyone six months and older should get the best available flu
protection, a flu vaccine every year.

Also available on YouTube  
_Running Time:_ 30 seconds

#### Flu Can Be Very Serious - Flu Vaccine Protects, 30-Second Spot

Flu can be very serious. A flu vaccine can keep you from getting sick with
flu, helping protect you, your loved ones, and the moments that matter most.
Everyone six months and older should get the best available flu protection, a
flu vaccine every year.

Also available on YouTube  
_Running Time:_ 30 seconds
