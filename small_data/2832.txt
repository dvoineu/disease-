# Public Health Associate Program (PHAP)

Minus

Related Pages

alert icon

#### The 2020 application period for PHAP candidates has closed. The
application period for PHAP host sites is now open. _Apply today!_

The Public Health Associate Program (PHAP) is a competitive, two-year, paid
training program with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. PHAP
associates are assigned to public health agencies and nongovernmental
organizations in the United States and US territories, and work alongside
other professionals across a variety of public health settings.

Throughout the two-year training program, associates gain hands-on experience
that will serve as a foundation for their public health careers. After
completing the program, PHAP graduates are qualified to apply for jobs with
public health agencies and organizations.
