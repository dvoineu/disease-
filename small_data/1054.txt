### About Vital Signs

CDC launched a program called CDC Vital Signs, which includes an MMWR Early
Release, a fact sheet and website, a media release, and a series of
announcements via social media tools.

Vital Signs will be released the first Tuesday of every month. Issues include
colorectal and breast cancer screening, obesity, alcohol and tobacco use, HIV
testing, seat belt use, cardiovascular disease, teen pregnancy and healthcare-
associated infections, asthma, and foodborne disease.
