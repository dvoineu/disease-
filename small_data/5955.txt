# Information for CDC-RFA-DP18-1803 State Public Health Approaches to
Addressing Arthritis

Minus

Related Pages

State Public Health Approaches to Addressing Arthritis, also known as the CDC-
RFA-DP18-1803, is a Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) for a 5-year
cooperative agreement to be supported by the Centers for Disease Control and
Prevention (CDC).

The NOFO's purpose is to implement state-based approaches to improve arthritis
management and quality of life for adults with arthritis.

call was conducted in a standard format that included a brief overview and a
question and answer session.

shared during the call, are available on the FAQ page.
