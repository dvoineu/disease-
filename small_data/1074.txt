# Parasites - _Dipylidium_ Infection (also known as Dog and Cat Flea Tapeworm)

Minus

Related Pages

![Dipylidium](/parasites/images/dipylidium/home_page_image_dipylidium.jpg)

_Dipylidium_ is tapeworm of cats and dogs. People become infected when they
accidentally swallow a flea infected with tapeworm larvae; most reported cases
involve children. _Dipylidium_ infection is easily treated in humans and
animals.

_Image: Left:_ D. caninum _egg packet, containing 8 visible eggs, in a wet
mount. Right: Adult tapeworm of_ D. caninum _. The scolex of the worm is very
narrow and the proglottids, as they mature, get larger. Credit:DPDx_
