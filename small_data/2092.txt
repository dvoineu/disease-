# Learn More About Hearing Loss in Children

EspaÃ±ol (Spanish)

Related Pages

Hearing loss can affect a child's ability to develop communication, language,
and social skills. The earlier children with hearing loss start getting
services, the more likely they are to reach their full potential. If you are a
parent and you suspect your child has hearing loss, trust your instincts and
speak with your child's doctor. Don't wait!
