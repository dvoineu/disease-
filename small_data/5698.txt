# Heart Disease Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Reports (MMWRs) About Heart
Disease

Minus

![MMWR](/heartdisease/images/hd-mmwr.jpg)

MMWR is a weekly scientific publication from CDC that reports public health
information and recommendations for the United States.

The following issues of _MMWR_ are relevant to heart disease. To locate
additional articles, you can use the CDC's advanced search feature.
