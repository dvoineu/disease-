participation in virtual events, we will update this calendar with the
details.**

Click on the highlighted date to see what event is happening that day.

Please note that a day without a blue box does not currently have an event.

The events listed in the hiring event calendar are not sponsored by CDC,
however, CDC may participate as a vendor at some events. For more information,
