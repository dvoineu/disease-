# Glanders

Minus

Related Pages

Glanders is an infectious disease that is caused by the bacterium
_Burkholderia mallei_. While people can get the disease, glanders is primarily
a disease affecting horses. It also affects donkeys and mules and can be
naturally contracted by other mammals such as goats, dogs, and cats.
