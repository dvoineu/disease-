Note

CDC does not see patients and is unable to diagnose your illness, provide
treatment, prescribe medication, or refer you to specialists.

If you have a medical emergency, please see your health care provider or the
nearest emergency room. If you are a health care provider, please contact your
state epidemiologist or local health department.
