### What is AFM?

Acute flaccid myelitis (AFM) is a rare but serious neurologic condition. It
affects the nervous system, specifically the area of the spinal cord called
gray matter, which causes the muscles and reflexes in the body to become weak.
We are working closely with national experts to better understand AFM and the
outbreaks.
