# Guidance for Travel

Minus

Related Pages

Travel to Arabian Peninsula

CDC does not recommend that anyone change travel plans because of MERS. The
CDC Yellow Book provides current travel health guidelines, including pretravel
vaccine recommendations, destination-specific health advice, and easy-to-
reference maps, tables, and charts.

Guidance for Airline Crew

Airline crew are asked to report ill travelers arriving from countries in and
near the Arabian Peninsula.
