# What CDC Does About Bird Flu

EspaÃ±ol

CDC works to improve global control and prevention of seasonal and novel
influenza, including avian influenza viruses, and works to improve influenza
pandemic preparedness and response. In collaboration with domestic and global
partners, CDC's Influenza Division pdf icon[1.1 MB, 2 pages]:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              * Builds surveillance and response capacity.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              * Monitors and assesses influenza viruses and illness.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              * Improves vaccines and other interventions.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              * Applies research to provide science-based enhancement of prevention and control policies and programs.
