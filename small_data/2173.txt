WISQARS Nonfatal Injury Data Visualization

CDC's WISQARS™ (Web-based Injury Statistics Query and Reporting System) is an
interactive, online database that provides fatal and nonfatal injury, violent
death, and cost of injury data from a variety of trusted sources. Researchers,
the media, public health professionals, and the public can use WISQARS™ data
to learn more about the public health and economic burden associated with
unintentional and violence-related injury in the United States.
