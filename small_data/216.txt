# Bacterial Vaginosis (BV)

Minus

Related Pages

![Any woman can get BV](/std/bv/images/bv-vp4-2.jpg)

Any woman can get BV.

Fact Sheet

![three women looking at laptop computer](/std/bv/images/factsheet.jpg)

Some women with BV don't know they have it because they have no symptoms.
Having BV can increase your chance of getting an STD.

More

Statistics

![women](/std/bv/images/statistics.jpg)

Bacterial vaginosis is the most common vaginal condition in women ages 15-44.

More

Treatment

![doctor and woman](/std/bv/images/treatment.jpg)

Treatment is especially important for pregnant women. Pregnant women with BV
may deliver premature (early) or low birth-weight babies.
