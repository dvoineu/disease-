# Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Reports About Stroke

Minus

Related Pages

![MMWR](/stroke/images/mmwr.jpg)

_MMWR_  is a weekly scientific publication from CDC that reports public health
information and recommendations for the United States.

The following stroke articles appeared in past issues of  _MMWR_. You can also
use CDC's advanced search feature to find additional articles.
